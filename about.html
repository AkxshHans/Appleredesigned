Apple Inc. is a multinational technology company headquartered in Cupertino, California, known for its
innovation, design, and consumer electronics products. Founded by Steve Jobs, Steve Wozniak, and
Ronald Wayne in 1976, Apple has grown to become one of the most valuable and influential companies
in the world.

Products and Services: Apple is renowned for its diverse portfolio of products, including the iPhone,
iPad, Mac computers, Apple Watch, and Apple TV. The company also offers software services such as
iOS, macOS, watchOS, and tvOS, as well as digital services like Apple Music, iCloud, and the App Store.
Design and Innovation: Apple is synonymous with cutting-edge design and innovation. Its products
are known for their sleek aesthetics, intuitive user interfaces, and groundbreaking features. The
company has a strong focus on research and development, continually pushing the boundaries of
technology to deliver new and improved products to consumers.
